Collaborations

Send request


As a writer, you can browse illustrator profiles in the app. If you find someone you'd like to work with, simply click the Request Collaboration button on their profile. The system will automatically send a request to the selected illustrator, notifying them of your interest in collaborating on a project.

Send postulation


Illustrators can submit their application for the project of their choice. Writers can access their application inbox to view all received contributions. Each new application will appear with a Pending status, allowing the illustrator to review it and decide whether to accept or reject the writer's proposal.

Our mission




Promoting visual artists

We promote the growth and visibility of visual artists through a specialized digital platform that allows them to share and monetize their illustrated works.


Innovative formats

Promote access to and dissemination of narrative content in innovative formats, such as illustrated books and comics.
